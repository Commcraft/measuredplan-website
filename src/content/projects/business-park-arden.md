A suite-by-suite area and square-footage study for a multi-building commercial business park in Arden. We documented each unit's footprint and heated area and compiled them into a clear schedule, giving the owner defensible numbers for leasing and management across the whole park. The work pairs a site plan of the building footprints with a room schedule tied to each suite.
