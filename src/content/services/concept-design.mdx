Concept design is the thinking stage: testing a brief against a site, working
out layout and massing, and settling on a direction before the expense of a
full construction set.

We keep it practical and buildable - concepts developed on an accurate base and
drawn so they carry cleanly through to construction drawings. Serving
homebuilders and owners across Western North Carolina.
