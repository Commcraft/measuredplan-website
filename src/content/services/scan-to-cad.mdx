A point cloud is only useful once it becomes drawings people can work from.
Scan-to-CAD is that step: registering the scan and drafting clean, layered 2D
CAD traced to what was actually captured.

It suits complex, detailed or hard-to-measure buildings, and it works whether we
capture the scan for you or you already have a point cloud. Serving Asheville and
Western North Carolina. See also [3D laser scanning](/services/3d-scanning/)
for the on-site capture.
