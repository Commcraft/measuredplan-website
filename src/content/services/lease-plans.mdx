A lease exhibit has to be unambiguous. The extent of the space and its measured
area both need to be right, because the exhibit becomes part of the lease.

We confirm the extent with you, measure where needed, and prepare a clear
exhibit - demising lines drawn, area calculated, everything labeled - ready to
attach to the agreement.
