Lease plans need to be unambiguous. The extent of the demise, the scale and the
compliance notes all have to be right, because the plan becomes part of a legal
document.

We confirm the extent with you, measure where needed, and prepare a plan that is
ready to register — clearly outlined, correctly scaled and properly referenced.
