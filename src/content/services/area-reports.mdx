Floor areas only mean something if the basis of measurement is clear. The same
building measures differently under GIA, NIA and IPMS, so the standard has to be
stated and applied consistently.

We confirm the standard, measure on site, and issue a short report with a
supporting plan, so the figures are transparent and easy to check.
