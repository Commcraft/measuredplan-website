Not every project needs a site visit - sometimes you already have the
information, it just isn't in usable CAD. CAD drafting takes what you have and
rebuilds it cleanly: correct layers, consistent line weights, a proper title
block, ready to edit.

If the source material is reliable, the drawing will be too. Where something in
the source is unclear or contradictory, we flag it rather than guess.
